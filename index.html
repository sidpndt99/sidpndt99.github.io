Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Crafted with Mastery

Shrigovind Trading Co. is a foundry and sculpture house based in Raisen, Madhya Pradesh — dedicated to creating exceptional statues and sculptures that stand as timeless works of art.

With over 25 years of experience, we have crafted and supplied more than 3,000 statues across diverse regions. Each piece is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans, reflecting artistic mastery and a profound respect for history and culture.
